There once was a young monkey named Harrison. Harrison grew up with another pet named Casey and was a German Shepard.

They are roughly the same age and they play together everyday. As they grew older, they noticed they needed money to buy more snacks to have around the house.

Harrison and Casey began to compete in local rodeos in their hometown that was located in Texas. In a couple months, they became a hometown favorite and managed to get enough money to buy as many snacks as they wanted.

They were approached by famous cowboys and were asked to compete in the statewide Texas rodeo in the Dallas Cowboys stadium.
